Grain game
Make a class called Grain. 
Then make a subclass called Rice that inherits the properties of the class Grain.
Repeat it with Wheat and Corn.
After this, make a method called grow() that will ask you to enter the type of grain, the time of the growing process
and the amount of water per day(the required amount will be displayed for easier reference).
After this, output whether your grain will end up in a good condition or a bad condition(in %).
Create a second method called harvest(), which you will be able to call in case of a good condition of the output of the grow() method.
Should the condition of the grain be bad, try again next year and suffer material losses.
